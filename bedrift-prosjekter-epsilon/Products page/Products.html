Flavours

Cola-Cherry

Epsilon's Cola-Cherry soda is a deliciously refreshing drink that offers
a unique twist on the classic cola flavor. The fusion of tangy cherry and
smooth cola creates a flavor explosion that is both sweet and tart,
leaving you with a pleasant aftertaste that will leave you wanting more.
Perfect for any occasion, Epsilon's Cola-Cherry soda is a must-try
for anyone looking for a new and exciting soda experience.

Grape

Epsilon's Grape soda is a deliciously sweet and fruity beverage
that will tantalize your taste buds. Made with the finest ingredients,
our grape soda is bursting with the authentic flavor of juicy grapes,
creating a refreshing and satisfying drink that is perfect for any occasion.

Guava

Epsilon's Guava soda is a tropical and refreshing beverage that will transport
you to a paradise island with every sip. Made with the highest quality guava
extract, our soda offers a unique blend of sweet and tangy flavours that are
sure to delight your taste buds. If you're looking for a new and exciting soda
experience, try Epsilon's Guava soda today and discover a taste of the tropics
in every sip.

Original

Epsilon's Original soda is a classic and timeless beverage that has been
enjoyed for generations. Made with a unique blend of natural flavors
and ingredients, our soda has a crisp and refreshing taste that is
perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a thirst-quencher
on a hot day or a tasty beverage to enjoy with friends, Epsilon's Original
soda is the perfect choice.
